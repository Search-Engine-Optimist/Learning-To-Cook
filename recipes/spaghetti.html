Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 400g dried spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Soften onion in oil; add garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes; simmer.
  3. Cook spaghetti; reserve a little water.
  4. Toss together; season to taste.
