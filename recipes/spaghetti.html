Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400g dried spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 400g beef mince
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée, salt & pepper

Method

  1. Heat oil, soften onion (5 min). Add garlic (1 min).
  2. Add mince; brown (5–7 min). Stir in tomatoes & purée; simmer 20 min.
  3. Boil spaghetti per pack, reserve ½ cup pasta water.
  4. Toss sauce with pasta; loosen with pasta water as needed. Season.
